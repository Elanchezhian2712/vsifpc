About Project

Our project's mission is to revolutionize accessibility for physically disabled individuals, including those who are deaf, blind, or mute. We are harnessing the power of cutting-edge AI technology, combining image and speech processing, to facilitate seamless real-time communication between disabled individuals and their desktop or laptop devices. Our innovation empowers and enriches lives, transcending barriers and fostering inclusivity for all.

Project

Virtual Smart Interaction for Physically Challenged

Problem Statement

"In a digital era, the disabled, including the deaf, blind, and mute, grapple with technology's accessibility challenges. Conventional interfaces fall short, causing isolation and hampering communication and productivity. To bridge this divide, we deploy cutting-edge AI to revolutionize accessibility, empowering all to engage seamlessly in the digital realm."

Our Solution

Our solution comprises four key components to enhance digital accessibility for individuals with physical disabilities


  • Virtual Eye Mouse: Eye-controlled computer navigation.
  • Voice Assistant: Hands-free device control and automation.
  • Virtual Hand Mouse: Gesture-based cursor control.
  • Virtual Keyboard: Typing with hand or eye movements.
